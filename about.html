WHAT IM INTRESETD IN

Artificial Inteligence

Artificial inteligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.

Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement Learning is a branch of Machine Learning where we have an actor who takes actions in an enviroment and gets rewards based on them. The actors task is to maximize it's cumulative reward.

Metaheuristics

Metaheuristics is a family of algorithms who's job isn't to find the best possible solution but to improve an existing one.
